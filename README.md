# 🕸️ Spider Chart Generator

A professional, interactive, and highly customizable **Radar Chart** creation tool. Designed for developers, data analysts, and professionals who need to visualize technical profiles or skill sets with elegance and precision.

## ✨ Key Features

* **⚡ Real-Time Visualization**: See changes instantly as you type skill names or adjust values.
* **📐 Dynamic Scaling**: Fully adjustable scale limits (0 to N) to fit any scoring system.
* **🎨 Advanced Customization**:
  * **10 Color Palettes**: From *Cyberpunk Neon* to *Deep Ocean* and *Ghost Minimalist*.
  * **Structural Styles**: Choose between **Solid lines**, **Large points** (Bolas), or **Dashed lines** (Rayas).
* **✍️ Multiline Titles**: Full support for professional, multi-line headers via the `Enter` key.
* **💾 Project Persistence**: Save your projects locally. Your designs are stored in your browser's `LocalStorage` and won't be lost on refresh.
* **📸 High-Quality Export**: Download your final chart as a high-resolution `.png` file ready for your CV or presentations.

---

## 🛠️ Built With

* **[Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/)**: The industry standard for flexible and interactive data visualization.
* **HTML5/CSS3**: Modern layouts with a focus on dark-mode aesthetics.
* **Vanilla JavaScript**: Optimized, dependency-free logic for lightning-fast performance.



---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Define your Skills**: Use the input panel to add, remove, or rename your technical skills.
2. **Set the Score**: Enter values based on your proficiency.
3. **Choose your Vibe**: Open the **Personalize Design** menu to mix colors and structural styles.
4. **Save/Load**: Use the orange **Save** button to keep track of multiple profiles.
5. **Export**: Click **Download PNG** to get your visual result.
